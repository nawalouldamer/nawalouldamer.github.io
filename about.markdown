## Interests

- Information retrieval and retrieval-augmented generation
- Uncertainty quantification and calibration for LLMs
- Reliability and evaluation of AI systems under real-world constraints
- Mechanistic interpretability — understanding model behavior from the inside

## Outside of work

I'm building a personal multi-agent assistant, currently just for myself, 
running locally, that helps me track and connect things I'd otherwise forget 
(food, training, ideas, ... etc). It's also my playground for applying uncertainty 
quantification ideas to a real system: when should an agent ask for clarification vs just log its best guess? I'm planning to open it up for 
others to test once it's further along.
